Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12 July 2026

Poly is a small, independently-run project. This page explains what data it collects, why, and how you can access, export, or delete it — in plain language, not legalese.

What's collected

DataWhy
Email, hashed password, usernameAccount creation and sign-in
Bio, avatar, bio photos, badgesYour public profile
Published packages, download countsThe community registry
Follows, friends, notificationsSocial features, opt-out per type in account settings
Hashed personal access tokensCLI and AI assistant (MCP) sign-in, revocable any time
Browser push subscription endpointReal OS notifications, only if you opt in; revocable any time
Payment confirmation (not full card details)Poly Pro one-time purchase, handled entirely by Stripe

Who processes it

Poly doesn't run its own servers. It relies on a small number of infrastructure providers:

  • Supabase — database, authentication, file storage, and the backend functions that power the site, CLI, and MCP server.
  • Stripe — payment processing for the one-time Pro purchase. Poly never sees or stores your card details.
  • Hostinger — hosts the static website (poly.candygate.eu).
  • GitHub — hosts the open source code and CLI release downloads.

Cookies and local storage

No advertising or tracking cookies. The site stores a couple of small preferences in your browser's local storage — theme (dark/light) and language — and Supabase stores your sign-in session so you don't have to log in on every page. Nothing is shared with ad networks or analytics trackers.

Retention and deletion

Your data is kept while your account is active. You can permanently delete your account and all associated data (profile, packages, tokens, social graph, notifications) from your account settings at any time — this can't be undone. You can also email open.corp.2030@gmail.com to request deletion or an export of your data.

Your rights

If you're in the EU/EEA or a similar jurisdiction, you have the right to access, correct, export, and delete your personal data, and to object to specific processing (like optional notifications). Most of this is self-service in account settings; anything else, email us.

Children

Poly isn't directed at children and shouldn't be used by anyone under 16.

Changes

This policy may be updated as the product evolves. Meaningful changes will be reflected here with an updated date at the top.

Contact

Questions, exports, or deletion requests: open.corp.2030@gmail.com. See also the Terms of Service.
